Easy and Best Exercises.!!

WALKING

Walking is a great way to improve or maintain your overall health. It is low impact,
requires minimal equipment, can be done at any time of day and can be performed at your own pace. It increases cardiovascular and pulmonary (heart and lung) fitness and reduces the risk of heart disease and stroke

CYCLING

Cycling can help to protect you from serious diseases such as stroke, heart attack,
some cancers, depression, diabetes, obesity and arthritis. It is a healthy, low-impact exercise that can be enjoyed by people of all ages, from young children to older adults. It is also fun, cheap and good
for the environment.
>

SKIPPING

Skipping helps improve coordination. It can build and maintain
bone density.Skipping increases running dpeed and may help ease anxiety.The benefits of
skipping rope include flexibility and agility. The muscles are greatly strengthened and relaxed as you jump.
